Special events

Energetica exhibition

We are thrilled to announce that the worldwide known Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Center this fall. The exhibition is on loan from The NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam and will be on display in Trondheim from th 22nd of september to the 30th of november.

The Energetica exhibition is a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering lighting, to ‘Wind Island’ that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.

Professor of Aeronautics

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’.

Night in the museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science
