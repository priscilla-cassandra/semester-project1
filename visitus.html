Visit us

Address:
Kongens gate 1
7011 Trondheim

Parking

Located 100m from the entrance we have a large parking lot with 200 parking spaces. Closest to the entrance we have 10 handicap-parking spaces. We also have chargers for electric cars so you can explore our museum and charge your car at the same time. The parking spaces are free of charge, but you do need to pay for charging.

By bus

Bus number 25 will take you near the science museum. The bus stop is only a 5-minute walk from the museum. Check out AtB for prices and departure times.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
